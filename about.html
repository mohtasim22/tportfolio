About Me

Hello, my name is Mohtasim Fahim. I am a Front End Web Developer living in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am working remotely on projects of clients all over the world through online market places.

I work to make a better web; one that is fast, easy to use, beautiful, accessible to all, and frustration-free. Regardless of your specific business requirements, in solving these challenges, you have a great chance of finding success online.

Work Process

Research and Plan

To start building anything in life it’s helpful to have a plan or idea of what you want to build and how you want it to look. It’s great to start with the purpose in mind, or how Simon Sinek would say — “Start With Why”.

Design and Develop

After understanding the purpose and planning a rough outline it’s time to think about design and develop! I’m a very visual person so it is definitely one of my favorite parts.

Deliver

After Research and plan then design and development it's time for delivery! It's important as it's the final representation of my efforts and it has to meet my clients expectations.
